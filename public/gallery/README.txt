PHOTO GALLERY INSTRUCTIONS
==========================

Add your photos to this folder!

RECOMMENDED:
- 12 photos for a balanced gallery layout
- Mix of portrait and landscape orientations for visual interest

NAMING CONVENTION:
- photo-01.webp
- photo-02.webp
- photo-03.webp
- ... up to photo-12.webp

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- WebP (recommended for best performance)
- JPG/JPEG
- PNG

TIPS:
- For best results, use WebP format (smaller file size, faster loading)
- Aim for images around 1200px wide
- The gallery will display placeholder boxes until you add your photos

To convert images to WebP, you can use online tools like:
- squoosh.app
- cloudconvert.com

After adding photos, you may need to restart the dev server.
